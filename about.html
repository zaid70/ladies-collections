About Ladies Collections

Founded in 2007 by Sabiha Rahman and established in the heart of Koramangala, Bangalore, Ladies Collections (LC Shop) has been a trusted name in premium jewelry for over two decades. We specialize in exquisite one-gram gold, antique, and sterling silver jewelry that celebrates the beauty and tradition of Indian craftsmanship.

Our curated collection features handpicked designs ranging from traditional temple jewelry to contemporary pieces, ensuring every woman finds something that resonates with her personal style. With over 20 years of expertise, each piece in our collection undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure exceptional craftsmanship.

At Ladies Collections, Sabiha Rahman personally addresses every customer, providing individual attention to help you find the perfect piece. We believe jewelry is not just an accessory but an expression of your personality and cherished memories. Visit our store to experience personalized service and the finest in jewelry craftsmanship.
